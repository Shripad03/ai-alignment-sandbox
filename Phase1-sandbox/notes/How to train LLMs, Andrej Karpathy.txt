How to train LLMs (Absolute begginer)

 - 2 stages while training LLMs.
1. Pre-stagin
   - Grab a huge data from the interneter(~10 TB of text)
   - Get a cluster of GPUs. ~6000
   - Compress text into neural nets, takes around 12 days and $2M.
This is you BASE MODEL.

2. Fine Tuning
   - Write labeling instruction.
   - Hire a people/scale.ai, collect 100k high quality valuable QAs, and/or comparisons.
   - wait for ~1 day, You get ASSISTANT MODEL.
   - Run evaluations, deploy.
   - Monitor, collect misbehavors, goto step1. reapeat.
